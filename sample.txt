Welcome to Mario's Italian Restaurant

About Us
Mario's Italian Restaurant has been serving authentic Italian cuisine since 1995. Located in the heart of downtown, we pride ourselves on using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients and traditional family recipes passed down through three generations. Our head chef, Marco Rossi, trained in Naples and brings over 20 years of culinary expertise to every dish.

Reservations
We accept reservations for parties of any size. For groups of 8 or more, please call at least 48 hours in advance. Walk-ins are always welcome but wait times may vary during peak hours (6-8 PM on weekends). You can make a reservation by calling (555) 123-4567 or through our website.

Delivery and Takeout
We offer delivery within a 5-mile radius. Minimum order for delivery is $25. Delivery fee is $3. Average delivery time is 30-45 minutes. All menu items are available for takeout. Order online or call us directly.

Catering
We provide catering services for events of 20 to 200 guests. Our catering menu includes buffet-style options and plated dinner service. Please contact us at least one week in advance for catering inquiries. Custom menus are available upon request.

Allergen Information
All dishes can be modified for common allergies. Gluten-free pasta is available for an additional $2. Vegan cheese can be substituted in any pizza or pasta dish. Please inform your server of any allergies before ordering. A detailed allergen menu is available upon request.
